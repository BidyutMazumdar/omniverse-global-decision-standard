About OMNIVERSE™

OMNIVERSE™ is defined as a sovereign-neutral, mathematically formalized global decision intelligence standard designed to unify governance systems, artificial intelligence alignment, and multi-agent coordination within a deterministic framework.

The system establishes a universal decision layer enabling evaluation, certification, and optimization of decision processes across institutional, national, and global systems.
